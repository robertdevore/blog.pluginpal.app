About Plugin Pal

This blog is for WordPress developers who build with purpose - and want to do it better.

Whether you're grinding out client projects, launching your own plugin business, or just want to level up your skills, Plugin Pal exists to help you ship smarter** and build faster**, with AI in your toolkit and no fluff in your feed.

What We Cover

  • AI tools + workflows for WordPress developers
  • Plugin tutorials - from quick wins to full builds
  • Behind-the-scenes breakdowns of Plugin Pal's own plugins
  • Dev pitfalls and lessons that save you time and headaches
  • How to write better, cleaner, more marketable code
  • The occasional rant, teardown, or real-talk post - because this space needs it

This blog is where I'll give it away.
